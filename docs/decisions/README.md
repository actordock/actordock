# ADRs

## Hard constraint

For platform mechanics (runtime, snapshots, Pause/Suspend, Worker multiplexing, control-plane hot path): ADRs must state what Substrate does today and why Actordock diverges—or confirm alignment. **Closed-door platform designs without a Substrate check are not acceptable.**

Research-policy ADRs may diverge freely; still cite Substrate when the change touches shared platform assumptions.

## ADR template

## Title

ADR NNNN: Short decision name

## Status

Proposed | Accepted | Superseded

## Context

What forces the decision? For platform topics: what does Substrate do?

## Decision

What we chose.

## Consequences

What becomes easier or harder; follow-ups.
