# Paid Time Off (PTO) Accrual

Employees accrue paid time off based on tenure.

## Accrual rate

Each completed year of tenure earns **6 PTO days**. Accrual is linear: multiply years of tenure by 6.

| Tenure (years) | Total PTO days |
|----------------|----------------|
| 1              | 6              |
| 2              | 12             |
| 3              | 18             |

The accrual rate is fixed regardless of department. Part-time employees prorate by FTE.

## Request process

Submit PTO requests at least 5 business days in advance unless otherwise approved.
